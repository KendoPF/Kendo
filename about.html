About

Background This page was developed to give potential kendo students information that may be of help since the closure of the local Louth Kendo Club

I myself was one of the students at the Louth Dojo and since its closure I have been looking for a new dojo and trying to improve my skills.
My aim here is to save other students and potential students the work I had to do to to collect the information on this website.

If you have any further queries or suggestions for or about the webpage please use the enquiries form above.


About me I am in my 50s and have studied martial arts and fencing in various forms since my teens. I have a number of years experience and I am still learning.
While I have a passion for Kendo and have spent some time compiling the information on this website, I am in no way affiliated with any offical Kendo Body or club.
Thanks again for visiting the site.
